KEY FINDINGS:
====================================================================

1. Match-Winning Factors:
   - Teams score 909 more runs on average in winning matches
   - Strike rate is 6.41 points higher in wins
   - Better bowling economy correlates with victories

2. Top Performers:
   - Best Run Scorer: Joe Root (4,100 runs)
   - Best Wicket Taker: Pat Cummins (193 wickets)
   - Most Consistent: AB de Villiers (61.11 average)
   - Most Lethal Bowler: Rashid Khan

3. Team Performance:
   - Best Win %: South Africa (80%)
   - Best Batting Team: England (1672.50 avg runs)

4. Critical Success Factors:
   - High run totals
   - Aggressive batting (high strike rate)
   - Economical bowling
   - Consistent performance
   - Balanced team composition
